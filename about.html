About Us

PT. GRYA DATA INDONESIA

Grya Data is a Bali-based data center under PT. Grya Data Indonesia, committed to strengthening the IT ecosystem by providing advanced network solutions, colocation services, and secure infrastructure. Partnering with leading NAPs, we ensure reliable, high-performance services for businesses.

We continuously innovate to accelerate digital transformation, enabling businesses to scale efficiently and stay competitive. With a strong focus on security, excellence, and cutting-edge technology, Grya Data is your trusted partner for scalable IT solutions.
